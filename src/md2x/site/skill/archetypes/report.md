## Archetype: report

For a findings / status report — exec-readable, evidence-led, numbers up front.

**Shell:** sidebar. **Render mode:** blocks (default).

**Structure.** Lead with a `hero` (title + dek framing the period and subject), then an executive `summary` a busy reader can stop at. Immediately follow with a `kpi_strip` of the headline metrics and a row of key-finding `callout`s (the "so what"). Then the detailed sections in priority order, each pairing `prose` with a `chart` or `table` built from real figures. Use a `timeline` for trend-over-time and `quote` for stakeholder input. Sidebar nav: Summary / KPIs / Findings / each section.

**Favor.** `chart` everywhere a number tells a story (trends, breakdowns, comparisons) — drive it from the source's actual figures, with units and a caption.

**Avoid.** Never invent or round-away data, and don't let visuals imply trends the numbers don't support. Every KPI and chart must trace to a figure in the source.
