## Archetype: presentation

For talk-style decks — one idea per slide, big type, spoken aloud or clicked through.

**Shell:** deck. **Render mode:** hybrid (default).

**Structure.** Open with a title slide (`hero`: title, subtitle, presenter). Then one slide per idea — each slide is a single beat: a bold headline plus at most a short `prose` line, one `figure`/`chart`, or a tight `card_grid` (3 items max). Sequence slides as a narrative arc (hook → context → core points → takeaway). End on a clear closing/CTA slide. Minimal prose everywhere; let the headline and one visual carry each slide.

**Favor.** The `deck` artifact as the spine (keyboard nav, slide counter, progress). Drop `chart` for any number worth dramatizing and `svg-figure` for a conceptual slide.

**Avoid.** Don't crowd a slide with paragraphs, nested bullets, or multiple charts. If a slide needs scrolling, split it. Stay faithful to the source's claims and figures.
