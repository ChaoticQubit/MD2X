## Archetype: plan

For a project / technical plan — phases, data flow, risks, and the path chosen.

**Shell:** sidebar. **Render mode:** hybrid (default).

**Structure.** Open with a `summary` of the goal and the headline decision. Then a `timeline` of phases/milestones (dates or sequence, owners, deliverables). Follow with a data-flow `flowchart` of how the system or process moves, a risk `table` (risk / likelihood / impact / mitigation), and a side-by-side `comparison` of the approaches considered with the chosen one marked and justified. Add a `kpi_strip` or `chart` for targets/estimates. Sidebar nav: Summary / Timeline / Flow / Risks / Options.

**Favor.** `flowchart` for the data/control flow, `comparison` for approach trade-offs, and `chart` for effort/cost/timeline projections. Hybrid so the flow and comparison are interactive.

**Avoid.** Don't fabricate dates, estimates, or risks the source doesn't state; mark anything speculative as an assumption. Keep trade-offs honest, not one-sided.
