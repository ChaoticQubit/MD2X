## Archetype: flyer

For a single-purpose promo page — an event, announcement, or offer that fits on one scroll.

**Shell:** landing. **Render mode:** blocks (default).

**Structure.** Lead with one punchy `hero` — headline, one-line value, and a primary CTA right there (date/place/price if it's an event). Follow with a few bold, scannable sections: a `card_grid` of highlights or "what you get," a `kpi_strip` for the standout numbers, a `timeline` only if there's a schedule, and a `callout` for the key detail (deadline, location, RSVP). Repeat the CTA near the end. Keep it short — one scroll, high contrast, big type.

**Favor.** `clickable-flow` if there's a simple "how it works" / sign-up path, and `svg-figure` for a hero graphic or map. Default to blocks; reach for hybrid only when a single interactive element clearly earns its place.

**Avoid.** No wall of prose, no docs-style nav, no buried CTA. Keep every fact (dates, prices, names) exactly as the source states — don't invent details.
