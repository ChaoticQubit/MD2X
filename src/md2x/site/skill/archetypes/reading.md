## Archetype: reading

For calm, long-form reading meant for research and learning — essays, papers, deep notes.

**Shell:** sidebar. **Render mode:** blocks (default).

**Structure.** Lead with a `hero` carrying title + one-line dek, then a per-page mini-TOC (`collapsible` or a short `card_grid` of anchored section links). Body is a generous run of `prose` blocks with comfortable measure; promote section headings to anchors. Break dense passages with the occasional `figure`, `quote` for pulled lines, and a `table` only when data is genuinely tabular. Close with a `glossary` for key terms and quiet cross-links between related sections.

**Favor.** `svg-figure` to redraw a concept the prose describes, and `comparison` to set two ideas side by side. Keep interactivity rare — reading comes first.

**Avoid.** Don't chop prose into bullet confetti or stack KPI strips; flow and faithfulness to the source argument matter more than dashboards. Never invent citations, figures, or claims not in the source.
