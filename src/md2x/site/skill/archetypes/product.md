## Archetype: product

For a marketing-style product or feature page — sell the thing, show it working.

**Shell:** landing. **Render mode:** hybrid (default).

**Structure.** Open with a `hero` (headline value prop + primary CTA + a hero visual). Follow with a `card_grid` feature grid (icon, name, one-line benefit each), then alternating content/visual sections — copy on one side, a `figure`/`chart`/widget on the other, flipping sides as you go. Add a `kpi_strip` for proof metrics, a comparison section for tiers or "us vs. them," and `quote` blocks for testimonials. Close on a strong CTA section.

**Favor.** `comparison` for plan/competitor tables, `clickable-flow` for an interactive "how it works," and `chart` for results or growth. Use hybrid so one or two widgets make the product feel alive.

**Avoid.** Don't overload with widgets or hype past the source — keep claims, prices, and benefits faithful to the input. Don't bury the CTA.
